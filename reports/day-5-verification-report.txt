F. FINAL RELIABILITY STATEMENT
======================================================================

✅ PostgreSQL is the source of truth
   - INSERT ... ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING
   - Transient failure → bounded retry (15s/30s/60s)
   - Persistent failure → DLQ after 3 attempts

✅ Elasticsearch is derived/rebuildable
   - ES failure does NOT retry, does NOT go to DLQ
   - Reindex script rebuilds ES from PG

✅ Bounded retries prevent infinite loops
   - Max 3 attempts with exponential backoff
   - Poison messages → DLQ immediately

✅ Redis idempotency unchanged
   - SET NX with 24h TTL
